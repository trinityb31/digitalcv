Professional Life

She currently works for the Center for Design Thinking at Elon University at a Student catalyst. At the center she facilitates workshops to students and staff about Design Thinking Practices and ways for participants incorporate them into their professional and personal lives. She is also the Assessment lead who analyzes feedback from the workshops and impliments changes to improve the quality of the workshops.

She is also a Media Analytics Executive at the student run Live Oaks Communications Agency. Her clients include Blue Ribbon Diner and Village Grill where she creates reports using SEMRush to analyze their social media performance and suggest improvements.
